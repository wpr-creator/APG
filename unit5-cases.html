Required SCOTUS Cases
3 required cases · Select one for full case breakdown
1962
Equal Protection
Baker v. Carr
Are legislative apportionment questions justiciable by federal courts?
Yes — "one person, one vote" begins here. Courts CAN review redistricting. Landmark for voting rights.
1993
Equal Protection
Shaw v. Reno
Can race be the predominant factor in drawing congressional district lines?
No — racial gerrymandering violates Equal Protection even when designed to help minority voters.
2010
1st Amend.
Citizens United v. FEC
Can Congress limit independent political expenditures by corporations?
No — political spending is protected speech. Opened the door to Super PACs. Most controversial campaign finance ruling.
